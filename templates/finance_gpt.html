Question: Explain the concept of a credit card.

FinanceGPT: A credit card is a financial tool issued by banks or financial institutions that allows users to borrow funds up to a certain limit to make purchases or withdraw cash. Unlike debit cards, which draw money directly from a user's bank account, credit cards provide a line of credit that must be repaid, typically with interest if not paid in full by the due date.
